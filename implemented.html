About Us

Organization Background
About Green Journey(GJ):
The Green Journey (GJ) is an independent and non-profit local humanitarian organization, dedicated to advancing the food security and livelihood of the rural community; and enhance the climate change aspects in Rakhine state.
The Green Journey (GJ) is registered in 2019 (Registration nb-2/RAK/038), mandate to focus the lifesaving actions during the emergency period of the state as a primary intervention while the medium to long term goal is to link the livelihood sector with the climate resiliency. As a motivated LNGO, GJ is looking forward to work in hand in hand with the International organizations and other relative stakeholders in operating the dedicated mission, addressing the identified issues and developing the organization’s capacity in parallel.
Mission && Vision
GJ’s mandate is to work with three main long-term aspects -
I. Protecting natural assets and reducing the natural disaster risks by supporting natural-resources-dependent stakeholders and re-establishing natural stability to decrease climate change impact in Rakhine State.
II. Launching the climate-resilient sustainable livelihood activity.
III. Promoting meaningful agriculture.
GJ is also committed to intervening in prolonged emergency response ( such as relief activity, water and sanitation, MRE, child protection, etc.) for the affected population whenever the state faces lifesaving issues due to arms -conflict and natural disasters.
